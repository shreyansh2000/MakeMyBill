Little champ Dancing is a Dancing competition which is looking for the best Dancers. The competition is meant for young children, which judges the prodigious children on the basis of their Dancing skills, Thinking talent, creative ideas and versatility in performance.The Dancing audition will be held in multiple cities across India. In this competition all the childreen which are below from 12years can participate so register As soon as posible... Audition details are mentioned on the page
